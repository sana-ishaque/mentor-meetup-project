Frequently Asked Questions

How to remove the heel?

You will be amazed at the fact that sliding the heels and buckle them is so simple and fun! Here's how it works

Can all heels be mounted on all shoes?

Yes! As long as the shoes are in the same size range. ( our heel )

Can the shoes not be fitted with a heel?

No. However, it is possible to install a low heel that is comfortable to walk.

Why can't I make flat shoes?

This is currently impossible. Our shoes are arched to add comfort while wearing and we can't abandon this arch. However, the shoes can be fitted with a low heel that is suitable for walking and very comfortable.

Can I press the disassembly button while walking?

No. This button is inside the shoe and cannot be activated while walking.

Do I feel the button when I walk?

Most people won't, but if you can feel it, please contact customer service at customer-services@flatandinches.com We will help you solve this problem. Usually we recommend a silicone insole, which is suitable for almost everyone.

Is the heel divided?

No matter what, we pursue as simple as possible. You can use any heel on any side.

Is the arch on the shoe very flexible?

No, in fact, the opposite is true. Arched to prevent falls, so no matter what height of heel you choose, you can stand very steady.

Why can't I wear a heel outside the size range?

Because each heel height in a range is designed according to the size of the shoe. In fact, in the footwear industry, the 8 cm heel of the 35th shoe is slightly smaller than the 8 cm heel of the 42nd shoe. In order for our customers to wear the correct heel height, we have to adhere to this principle.
